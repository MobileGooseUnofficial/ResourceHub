This was made for DesktopGoose v0.3, however it works on Mobile Goose as well. Put the contents of "Goose Notepad" into /Library/MobileGoose/Notes. DO NOT PUT THE "Goose Notepad" FOLDER INTO THE Notes FOLDER OR IN /Library/MobileGoose. It will not work.
